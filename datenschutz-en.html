Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy

Effective Date: October 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Routine Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

We do NOT collect any personal data. Routine Tracker is designed with privacy first - all your data stays locally on your device.

1.2 Usage Data

We do not collect usage data, analytics, or any information about how you use the app.

1.3 Device Information

We do not access or collect device information, contacts, location, or any other device data.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, process, or share any of your data. All app functionality works locally on your device.

Your routine data, settings, and preferences are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure Android storage mechanisms. Your data never leaves your device.

3.2 No Cloud Backup

We do not provide cloud backup services. Your data remains private and under your control.

3.3 App Deletion

When you uninstall the app, all your data is automatically deleted from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Routine Tracker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

The app works completely offline and does not require an internet connection for any functionality.

5. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders for your routines (optional, can be disabled)
  • Storage: To save your routine data locally on your device

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and never for data collection.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children under 13.

Parents can safely let their children use Routine Tracker without privacy concerns.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."

Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. Please review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@routinetracker.app

Developer: Sebastian Rothe

Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

9. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any personal information:

  • Access: You have full access to all your data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your data anytime by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Portability: Your data is stored in a standard format on your device
  • Control: You have complete control over your data at all times

Privacy Summary

100% Private: No data collection, no tracking, no cloud storage. Your data stays on your device, always.
